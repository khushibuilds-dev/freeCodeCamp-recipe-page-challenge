My Coding Journey

Welcome to my coding journey! Here, I will share my experiences, projects, and insights as I learn and grow in the world of programming. Join me as I explore different languages, frameworks, and technologies, and document my progress along the way. After my intermediate studies, I initially aimed for a government job, but the need for a high-growth career led me to Web Development. When institutes told me my old laptop wouldn't be enough, I didn't give up. I turned to freeCodeCamp and Google to teach myself. Now, I am building my foundation as a developer, one project at a time, proving that passion is more important than expensive hardware. Welcome to my journey!
