Cauliflower's Dumplings

A traditional and savory North Indian delicacy 'Gobhi ki rasaj' is a popular winter specialty across the region.It is also known by other names such as gobhi ke fare.

Ingredients

  • fresh gobhi
  • gehu ka atta
  • water
  • ghee / tel
  • some common spices

Instructions

  1. Knead a frim dough by adding water gardually as needed.
  2. Wash the cauliflower , grate it finely ,and cook it into a dry subji according to your taste.
  3. make small balls of the dough , fill them with the dry subji, and shape them like a gujiya.
  4. heat oil in a kadhai and prepare a masala by sauteing onions , just like for a gravy dish.Add water and let it warm up, them drop the stuffed gujiya into it.
  5. after cooking for 10 to 15 minutes , turn off the heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
